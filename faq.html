Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SeZZamE.

Getting Started

Yes. SeZZamE is free to download from the App Store. Core alarm features — custom sounds, timezone alarms, multi-day plans, categories, iCloud sync, Live Activity, and more — are included at no cost. A Premium subscription ($0.99/month or $7.99/year) unlocks all color themes, trash restore, and additional features.
SeZZamE requires iOS 16.4 or later. It is optimised for iPhone and iPad. Features such as Live Activity require iOS 16.2+, which is included in the minimum requirement.
Yes. SeZZamE requires no account registration. It uses your existing Apple ID for iCloud sync (which every Apple device owner already has), but you can use the full app without enabling sync.
The built-in Clock app handles simple wake-up alarms. SeZZamE adds: timezone-aware alarms, multi-day planning, custom audio recording and import, alarm sharing via QR code or link, OCR import from Clock screenshots, iCloud sync across devices, motivation screens, location and link attachments, Live Activity, widgets, Apple Watch support, stopwatch with lap sharing, multiple running timers, color themes, and categories — none of which exist in the Clock app.

Alarms & Scheduling

Yes. Any alarm in SeZZamE can be pinned to a specific timezone. The alarm fires at the correct local moment in that zone regardless of where you are physically located. This is essential for remote meetings, international travel, and cross-timezone scheduling.
Plans are multi-day alarm schedules. Build a sequence of days, each with its own set of alarms and times. When your schedule shifts — say, a conference moves a day earlier — slide the entire Plan and all its alarms shift together. Plans support custom repeat patterns: weekly, monthly, quarterly, or yearly. Access Plans from the Plans tab.
Beyond the standard daily/weekly repeat, SeZZamE supports monthly, quarterly, and yearly recurrence. You can set alarms like "first Monday of every month" or "every quarter." Custom cron-style schedules give you precise control over any repeat pattern.
Yes. Any alarm can include a destination — saved as a named location. When the alarm fires, the location is visible so you immediately know where you need to be. You can tap to open it in Maps.

Sounds & Media

In the alarm detail screen, open the Sound section and choose "Record" to capture audio with your microphone, or "Import" to bring in an audio file from your device. Custom sounds are stored locally and synced via iCloud. The same custom sound can be reused across multiple alarms.
When an alarm fires, SeZZamE displays a full-screen view with a motivational message and background image you've chosen. Set the text and photo in the alarm detail screen. You can manage custom motivation photos from Settings → Manage Data → Motivation Photos.

Sharing & Import

Yes. SeZZamE generates a shareable link or QR code for any alarm or alarm set. The recipient opens the link or scans the code and SeZZamE recreates those alarms on their device. Ideal for syncing family schedules, team standups, or travel itineraries.
SeZZamE can read screenshots of the iOS Clock app using on-device OCR. Go to Settings → Manage Data → Import and select your screenshot. SeZZamE detects the alarm times and recreates them. All recognition happens locally — no data leaves your device.

iCloud & Data

Yes. SeZZamE uses Apple's CloudKit framework to sync alarms, plans, categories, and settings across all your Apple devices automatically. Your data lives in your private iCloud container — SeZZamE has no access to it.
Yes. iCloud via CloudKit provides automatic backup. You can also export individual alarms or full alarm sets as a file for manual backup or transfer.
Yes. All alarm scheduling and firing works entirely offline. iCloud sync runs when a connection is available but is never required for your alarms to fire correctly.

Premium

Premium unlocks: all color themes, the ability to restore deleted alarms from Trash, unlimited custom app links, and all future Premium-only features. The subscription is $0.99/month or $7.99/year (save over 30%). Both plans include a free trial period.
Open the iOS Settings app → your Apple ID → Subscriptions. Find SeZZamE and tap Cancel Subscription. Your Premium access continues until the end of the current billing period. No action is needed inside the SeZZamE app itself.
SeZZamE provides watch surfaces through widget complication support, making alarm context visible on your wrist. Live Activities also surface on the Dynamic Island and Lock Screen of your iPhone so alarm context is always in view.

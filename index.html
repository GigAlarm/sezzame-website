Features

Every alarm feature you've ever wanted

SeZZamE goes far beyond wake-up times. It's an alarm system designed for how real people live.

🔔

Timezone-Aware Alarms

Schedule alarms against any timezone — perfect for remote meetings, international travel, or cross-coast calls. The alarm fires at the right local moment no matter where you are.

📅

Multi-Day Plans

Build a full schedule spanning multiple days. Shift the entire plan when timing changes. Use custom repeat patterns — weekly, monthly, quarterly, or yearly.

🎵

Custom Audio

Record or import any audio file as your alarm sound. Reuse custom sounds across multiple alarms. Your alarm, your soundtrack.

📤

Share & Import Alarms

Share alarm sets with family or coworkers via QR code or link. Import alarm schedules from screenshots of the iOS Clock app using on-device OCR.

🖼️

Motivation Screen

Set a motivational message and background image that appears the moment your alarm fires. Start every morning with intent.

📍

Locations & Links

Attach a destination to any alarm so you know exactly where to go. Add meeting links, tickets, or event URLs for one-tap post-alarm action.

☁️

iCloud Sync

Your alarms sync seamlessly across all your Apple devices via CloudKit. Set it on iPhone, see it on iPad — everything stays in step.

🕐

Live Activity & Widgets

Keep alarm context visible on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island with Live Activities. Widget surfaces for your Home Screen and Apple Watch.

🎨

Color Themes

Choose a theme that matches your style and lighting. Multiple free themes included; Premium unlocks the full collection.

📁

Categories & Groups

Organize large alarm sets by category — Morning Routine, Work, Workout, and more. Scan and manage your schedule at a glance.

⏱️

Stopwatch & Timers

A persistent stopwatch with lap sharing, plus multiple running timers with custom end sounds — all inside the same app.

🔗

Custom App Links

Save app or web links you reuse often, then attach them to alarms for quick post-alarm action. Open Spotify, Maps, or your meeting tool in one tap.

🤝

Finally Feels Like Android

Switched from Android and miss Google Clock's power? SeZZamE brings the alarm depth Android users expect — custom sounds, flexible repeats, timezone control, and schedule sharing — natively on iPhone.
